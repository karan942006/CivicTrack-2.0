For a **Smart Municipality / CitizenOS** app, avoid the typical government look (dark blue + gray). Most hackathon teams build boring dashboards. You want something that feels:

* Professional
* Trustworthy
* Modern
* Premium
* Accessible
* Government-grade

# 🏆 Best Color Combination (Recommended)

### Primary Color

**Royal Blue**
`#2563EB`

Represents:

* Trust
* Governance
* Technology
* Reliability

### Secondary Color

**Emerald Green**
`#10B981`

Represents:

* Growth
* Sustainability
* Development
* SDGs

### Accent Color

**Amber**
`#F59E0B`

Used for:

* Notifications
* Warnings
* Important actions

### Error Color

`#EF4444`

### Background

`#F8FAFC`

### Surface Cards

`#FFFFFF`

### Text Primary

`#0F172A`

### Text Secondary

`#64748B`
