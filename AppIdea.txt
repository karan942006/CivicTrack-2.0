Based on the transcript, this is much more than a simple complaint app. It is a **Municipality Integrated Service System (Smart Municipality / Digital Governance Platform)** designed to digitally connect citizens, municipal officials, departments, and public services.

# 1. Complete App Vision

The app aims to become a **single digital platform for all municipal services** where citizens can:

* Access government information
* Submit complaints
* Contact officials
* Receive emergency support
* Suggest development ideas
* Access public notices
* Obtain recommendations/certificates online
* View municipality information
* Communicate with representatives

In simple terms:

> **"Everything related to the municipality in one mobile application."**
